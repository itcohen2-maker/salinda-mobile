# Background Prompts

Use these prompts for background exploration in Claude Design.

## 1. Gameplay Background

```text
Create a gameplay background for a premium mobile puzzle card game called "Hidden Rule".

Art direction:
- dark scholarly atmosphere
- calm, intelligent, tactile
- premium arcane puzzle
- not casino
- not fantasy RPG
- not glossy mobile casual

Look:
- deep charcoal base
- subtle ink-blue lift behind the card play area
- soft vignette around the edges
- extremely restrained texture, like fine paper dust or worn archival grain
- the center should support cards and buttons clearly
- the background should feel rich but quiet

Palette:
- #14161B
- #1C2128
- #232A33
- #2F3A4A
- #EDE4D3
- #A88A58

Constraints:
- no green felt table
- no sparkles
- no purple fantasy haze
- no heavy illustration
- no visual clutter

Output:
- mobile portrait background
- front-facing
- production-friendly
- cards must remain the heroes
```

## 2. Home Background

```text
Create a home screen background for a premium mobile puzzle card game called "Hidden Rule".

Mood:
- ceremonial
- elegant
- mysterious
- intelligent
- premium

Visual direction:
- dark archive desk atmosphere
- refined depth
- subtle material transitions
- quiet central stage for logo and CTA
- more dramatic than gameplay, but still restrained

Palette:
- charcoal
- ink blue
- soft brass
- ivory
- a very subtle oxblood hint if useful

Avoid:
- casino UI look
- arcade energy
- bright gradients
- fantasy symbols everywhere
- cluttered props

Output:
- mobile portrait background
- enough negative space for title and primary CTA
- premium puzzle product feel
```

## 3. Chapter Intro Background

```text
Create a chapter intro background for a premium mobile puzzle card game called "Hidden Rule".

The screen should feel like a ceremonial transition into a new chapter.

Mood:
- quiet tension
- archival mystery
- premium
- dramatic but controlled

Look:
- darker center stage
- subtle brass presence
- soft oxblood undertone allowed
- elegant falloff into charcoal darkness
- enough space for a chapter title and one premium CTA

Avoid:
- ornate fantasy overload
- glowing magic effects
- casino shine
- heavy objects or scenery

Output:
- mobile portrait background
- front-facing
- readable under title text and premium button
```
